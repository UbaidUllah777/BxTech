How to Work With Us?


We offer two types of different engagement models for our customers to choose from.

Fixed Price

  • Works efficiently on the projects with clear, stable, and settled requirements.
  • The cost of the project is fixed and discussed in the discovery phase or before the project starts.
  • Only pre-discussed 'milestones' of the project are charged, we expect the payment once it is finished.

Dedicated Teams

  • Works perfectly for projects that do not have clear requirements and are spread over long time.
  • Full control over the development process; significant cost-savings on infrastructure, social benefits, etc.
  • No hiring efforts, lower TCO, ability to quickly scale the team; Perfect for all types of projects.
