Our Process

  1. Requirements & Analysis

    This is where we chat, over a cup of coffee. Our team methodically pores over your requirements, and analyses the best possible solutions that fit your needs.

  2. Design

    Our design consists of wire frames or mockups. This is the initial prototype of the design we’ve finalized, to give you a taste of the end result. Your feedback is extremely important to us.

  3. Development

    The development process is where we really take a deep dive into your project. This is where we do what we love - coding!

  4. Testing

    Testing is an essential part of our process. We follow through with 4 types of testing:

    • Unit label testing
    • Integration
    • System label testing
    • Performance and usability tests
    • Security assessment tests

    We make sure everything's optimized and streamlined.

  5. Deployment

    This is the stage of execution. Deployment takes place in the target environment, and is carried out in accordance to the requirements of the client - either in the cloud, or a server. Our process is entirely client - centric, guaranteeing that you call the shots.
