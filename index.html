Our Engagment Model for Clients

We offer two types of engagement models for our customers to choose from; the basic intuition behind this approach is to provide more flexibility to our clients in selecting the suitable engagement model of their choice.

Fixed Price model

  • This model fits on projects with clear, stable and upfront requirements.
  • The project cost is determined and estimated during the discovery phase. However, late Requirement change is always accepted.
  • A slight disadvantage of this model is that only pre-decided features of the system are delivered to the customers.

Dedicated Resource Model

  • Dedicated teams and resource allocation model fits into the projects where the requirements and features are not clear upfront.
  • Complete control over the development process; major infrastructure cost saving, close interaction with the development team, and many other advantages.
  • No hiring effort is required at all, the TCO is cheaper, and the team can be scaled up fast, making it ideal for all projects.
