Our Engagment Model for Clients

We offer two types of engagement models for our customers to choose from. The basic intuition behind this approach is to offer more felxibility to our client in selecting the suitable engagment model of their choice

Fixed Price model

  • This model really fits on projects with clear, stable and upfront requirements.
  • The costing of the project is determined and estimated in the requirements analysis phase of the project.
  • A slight disadvantage of this model is that only pre-decided features of the system is delivered to the customers.

Dedicated Resource Model

  • Didicated teams and resource allocation model really fits into the projects where the requirements and features are not clear upfornt.
  • Complete control over the development process; major infrastructure cost savings; social advantages, etc.
  • No hiring effort is required, the TCO is cheaper, and the team can be scaled up fast, making it ideal for all projects.
